# L6: Engagement Engine

**Version:** 5.3.0
**Status:** ✅ Operational (Phase 5 Enterprise Scale)
**Phase:** Driver Experience / Enterprise Scale

## Overview

Gamification engine that drives driver engagement through leaderboards, achievements, and personalized challenges.

## Features

- **Leaderboards**: Fleet-wide and regional rankings
- **Achievements**: Unlockable badges and milestones
- **Challenges**: Time-limited bonus opportunities
- **Push Notifications**: Targeted engagement campaigns

## Gamification Mechanics

- **Green Driver Score**: Based on off-peak charging participation
- **Streak Tracking**: Consecutive days of optimal charging
- **Team Challenges**: Fleet vs. fleet competitions
